What is Assassins?

Assassins is a live-action elimination game. Each player is assigned a target at the beginning of the game. The goal is to kill your target before you are eliminated by the assassin hunting you. For our game, the weapons of choice are spoons, poison cards, and cheesing. Eventually, there will only be 2 assassins left. The winner is the last assassin standing.

How do I get started?

All of this game's administration will happen over SMS (barring a cataclysmic system malfunction). Once you register your phone, we will it information about your target, game status, and the day's safety item.
